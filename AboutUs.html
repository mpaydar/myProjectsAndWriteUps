About US

BM Software Solution is a software engineering studio dedicated to delivering practical, production-ready AI and backend systems. Founded by Mohammad Bayat, our mission is to transform advanced technologies into accessible, impactful tools for businesses of all sizes.

We’re not just a service provider — we’re building a brand. Our flagship product, a Smart Weight Scale, is paired with a beautifully designed mobile app that leverages user data to create a personalized, intelligent experience — the first of its kind in the market.

But this is just the beginning. At BM Software Solution, we’re committed to building industrial-grade applications and treat every client project with precision, care, and a deep respect for quality.
